ABOUT Amwelase (Pty) Ltd

Amwelase (Pty) Ltd, founded in 2014, is a 100% black-managed and owned South African company delivering professional services in civil works, building construction, and SHEQ management. Guided by our motto, "Rely on Our Ability", we are committed to offering safe, cost-effective, and high-quality project delivery to clients in both public and private sectors.

Our company empowers local communities by creating sustainable employment and continuously training our team to reach their full potential. We strictly follow SHEQ regulations and comply with the Occupational Health and Safety Act, ensuring safety, quality, and professionalism in every project we undertake.

Our Mission

To be a competitive, quality-driven organization that guarantees client satisfaction through personal attention, strong communication, and professional execution.

Our Vision

To bring excellence and competency to every client relationship while supporting continuous growth, leadership development, and community upliftment.

Our Values

  • Quality Assurance: Delivering exceptional workmanship and service to meet client expectations.
  • People Development: Uplifting and training our workforce to achieve their full potential.
  • Health & Safety: Maintaining a safe and healthy work environment under OHS regulations.
  • Integrity: Open communication and mutual respect in all operations and partnerships.
